# Feature 11.4: Legal & Support Pages

**Status:** Not Started
**Effort:** Medium
**Phase:** 11 - Marketing & Public Pages

## Overview

Required legal and support pages. This feature creates the essential legal documents and contact pages needed for business compliance and customer support.

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements
- About Us page with company information
- Contact page with form
- Terms of Service page
- Privacy Policy page
- Cookie Policy page
- Email notification for contact form submissions

### Business Rules
- Contact form submissions sent to support email
- Legal pages must comply with GDPR requirements
- Cookie policy must document all cookies used
- Terms must cover SaaS-specific provisions

## Files

### Created/Modified
- `resources/views/public/about.blade.php` - About Us page
- `resources/views/public/contact.blade.php` - Contact page with form
- `resources/views/public/terms.blade.php` - Terms of Service
- `resources/views/public/privacy.blade.php` - Privacy Policy
- `resources/views/public/cookies.blade.php` - Cookie Policy
- `app/Http/Controllers/ContactController.php` - Contact form handler

### Routes
```php
Route::get('/about', function () {
    return view('public.about');
})->name('about');

Route::get('/contact', [ContactController::class, 'show'])
    ->name('contact');
Route::post('/contact', [ContactController::class, 'submit'])
    ->name('contact.submit');

Route::get('/terms', function () {
    return view('public.terms');
})->name('terms');

Route::get('/privacy', function () {
    return view('public.privacy');
})->name('privacy');

Route::get('/cookies', function () {
    return view('public.cookies');
})->name('cookies');
```
