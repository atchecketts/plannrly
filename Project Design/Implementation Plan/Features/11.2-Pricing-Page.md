# Feature 11.2: Pricing Page

**Status:** Not Started
**Effort:** Medium
**Phase:** 11 - Marketing & Public Pages

## Overview

Detailed pricing with feature comparison. This page provides comprehensive pricing information including tier comparisons, add-on options, and enterprise inquiry capabilities.

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements
- Monthly/annual toggle with savings shown
- Three main tiers (Starter, Professional, Enterprise)
- Feature comparison table
- Add-on pricing section
- FAQ specific to pricing
- Contact for enterprise custom pricing

### Technical Requirements
- Mobile responsive pricing cards
- Toggle between billing periods
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Accessible pricing tables
